About

Curious across disciplines.
Precise in the work.

I am a Cybersecurity Engineering MSc student at Politecnico di Torino and a Master's thesis researcher collaborating with Rakuten. Before specializing in security, I trained as a computer engineer-a foundation that still shapes how I move between software, systems, data, and human-centered design.

I am drawn to projects that require more than one lens: exploit research grounded in reproducible evidence, educational software that evaluates reasoning rather than clicks, and machine learning examined for both performance and failure modes. I care about the craft of explaining technical work as much as making it work.

I also like ambitious work to feel human. I value teams that can stay exacting about the result without becoming solemn about the process-an outlook I bring to research, hackathons, and long project days alike.
